🎯 RSI - Relative Strength Index

What it shows: Whether gold is overbought or oversold. How to read: Above 70 = Overbought (may drop soon), Below 30 = Oversold (may rise soon), 30-70 = Normal range.

RSI Value: Momentum indicator (0-100)
Overbought Zone: Above 70 (consider selling)
Oversold Zone: Below 30 (consider buying)

⚡ MACD - Trend Momentum

What it shows: Strength and direction of price momentum. How to read: Green bars = Positive momentum (bullish), Red bars = Negative momentum (bearish). Taller bars = Stronger momentum.

Positive MACD: Bullish momentum (price likely to rise)
Negative MACD: Bearish momentum (price likely to fall)
